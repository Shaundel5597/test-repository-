The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is a distinct style of garden that uses an informal design, dense paintings, and a mixture of ornamental and edible plants.

The Cottage Garden originated in England and its history can be taced back for centuries, although they were reinvented in 1870's England, when stylized versions were formed as a reaction to the more structured and rigurously maintained English estate gardens.

The earliest cottage gardens were more practical than their modern descendants,with an emphasis on vegetables and herbs, along with some fruit trees.
